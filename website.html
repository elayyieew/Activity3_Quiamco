About Crochet


Tradition since 1889

Crochet is dedicated to bringing warmth and charm into your life through handcrafted crochet creations. Our team of skilled artisans meticulously crafts each item with love and attention to detail. Whether you're searching for cozy home decor, stylish accessories, or unique gifts, we have something special for everyone.

Crochet Mignon traces its roots back to 2010 when founder Elay embarked on a journey of creativity and warmth. With a passion for crochet deeply embedded in her soul, Elay began experimenting with yarn and hook in the comfort of her own home. Driven by a desire to create something truly special, she poured her heart and soul into each stitch, weaving together strands of yarn to form intricate patterns and designs.
